FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our salon, color disciplines, and booking process.

A 15-minute diagnostic session where Mercedes analyzes your hair's history, current structural integrity, and goals. We establish a realistic roadmap and custom formula plan before starting any chemical work.

Premium balayage and highlights typically require 3 to 4 hours, depending on hair length and density. Complex color corrections can take 4 to 6 hours as we prioritize hair health and perform progressive lightening stages.

Yes. Every single lightening and coloring service includes advanced bond-building therapy (like Olaplex) built directly into the formulation to protect, reconstruct, and restore your hair's structural bonds.

We recommend coming with clean, dry hair. Please avoid heavy root touch-up sprays or waxes. Prepare a list of your hair color history from the last 2 years, including box dyes, as this influences chemical formulations.
