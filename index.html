Our Story

Welcome to Mercedes Salon

For years, Mercedes Salon has been the premier destination for beauty and grooming in Baytown, Texas. Founded by Mercedes, our salon was built on a passion for creative expression, personalized client care, and the belief that everyone deserves to look and feel absolute best.

We combine years of professional experience with a warm, welcoming environment. Whether you are looking for a complete makeover, a vibrant new hair color, precision highlights, or professional grooming, our dedicated team of stylists, barbers, and nail technicians is here to craft a customized style that highlights your unique features.
